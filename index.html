SANE a game for allyship and justice

What is SANE?

SANE is both an acronym for the names of the game creators and a philosophy for helping others. To keep your community "sane," you must defend its members from harassment and microaggressions.

In this game, you are a social justice hero, going around your city to help people being discriminated against because of their identity. Choose the correct dialogues and try to win the most points!

How to Play:

Click on the green "Start" button to play, then click on the game's window.
Hold the arrow keys to move the avatar around the screen.
Hit the spacebar to advance through dialogue.
Press the number keys to choose what to say.
When you finish one level, click on the green button again to move on to the next one. Right now the available levels are:

  • Level 1: Black Lives Matter
  • Level 2: Breastfeeding and Menstruation Stigmas
  • Level 3: Islamophobia
  • Level 4: Catcalling and Street Harassment
